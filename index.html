    The Art of the Pen

    At Scriptorium Collective, we believe that every stroke holds a story. Nestled in our Pacific Street studio in Brooklyn, our artisanal approach merges time-honored script techniques with contemporary design aesthetics. We cherish the quiet discipline of the hand-crafted, bringing soulful precision to every project.

    Our illuminated manuscripts, imbued with the glow of 24k gold, and striking custom event signage are meticulously tailored to create truly immersive experiences. We strive to bring the tactile beauty of ink and precious metals into an increasingly digital age, offering a tangible connection to artistry and elegance.

    Master the Script: Calligraphy Workshops

    Join our comprehensive classes, strictly limited to 8 seats per session, held in our inspiring New York studio to ensure personalized attention.

    Introduction to Pointed Pen

    $125

    A 3-hour foundational course covering nib pressure, basic strokes, and alphabet mapping for beginner enthusiasts. All high-quality materials are provided, setting you on your calligraphic journey.

    Copperplate Mastery

    $250

    An intensive 2-day weekend workshop focusing on the elegant flourishing of Copperplate script structure and traditional ink formulation. Elevate your skills with advanced techniques.

    $380

    An advanced masterclass dedicating itself to the ancient art of gilding techniques using classical breathing tubes and natural gum ammoniac. Discover the magic of gold.
